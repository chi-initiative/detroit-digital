Detroit has always been perceived to fit certain ideals, historically and in recent years; but, these stereotypes do not always conform to the rich heritage of the city. There are many ways of “knowing” Detroit: through looking at the physical layout and design of the city itself, through people speaking out about community development aimed to form a positive perception of identity, and through listening to the popular cultures’ interpretation of Detroit as a whole.
